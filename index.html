Project Positioning

Built for practical Indian commercial spaces

Instead of replacing infrastructure, SmartTherm makes existing rooms smarter through sensors, automation logic, and a live dashboard.

The Problem

Commercial rooms often waste electricity because ACs, lights, and fans keep running without awareness of actual occupancy, temperature, humidity, or load conditions.

  • Appliances running in empty rooms
  • No live visibility into energy-heavy zones
  • Manual control causing comfort and cost imbalance

Proposed Solution

SmartTherm uses ESP32-based sensing, non-invasive monitoring, IR control, smart relays, and dashboard intelligence to make appliance behavior adaptive instead of fixed.

  • Senses activity and environmental conditions
  • Optimizes comfort and energy together
  • Works as a retrofit layer over existing appliances

Thermal Mapping

The dashboard converts invisible room conditions into a live visual state, showing whether the prototype room is safe, warming, or outside the ideal comfort band.

Comfort Score

A real-time score blends temperature, humidity, occupancy, power usage, and AC state so comfort and efficiency can be judged together.

Impact

Smarter comfort, lower wastage

SmartTherm is positioned as a low-cost, retrofit-ready adaptive energy layer for offices, schools, co-working spaces, hotels, hospitals, and other shared environments.
